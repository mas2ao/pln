 Aramyan LEI Wageningen University and Research Centre The Hague The Netherlands Abstract Purpose Chinese agri food chains consist of the millions of small scale farmers who are not well structured and organized in the supply chain;; Therefore the purpose of this paper is to develop a theoretical framework for supply chain governance including its antecedents and consequences as well as a series of hypotheses for empirical testing;; In agri food supply chain production process requires investments which are mostly sunk costs because assets cannot be easily converted or used for other purposes therefore they are specific Bijman 2002 ;; The purpose of governance mechanism is to provide at minimum costs the coordination control and trust that are necessary for chain actors to believe that engaging in the exchange will make them better off Williamson 1985 ;;
